Bacon And Eggs Breakfast

The 100% American Breakfast.

As American as Apple Pie, gun culture and imperialism, bacon and eggs will leave you measuring your height using inches and feet as if it was something natural to you. Charged with protein, this simple and fast recipe will make you want to drop some tea on the nearest river while you yell Freedom!!! like a mad person.

In order to make them you'll need all the ingredients listed below:

  • 2 medium eggs
  • 30g rindless back bacon
  • 33g white bread
  • Freedom

Now let's get on to it. Follow the instructions listed below and make Abraham Lincoln proud.

  1. Turn the grill on to a high heat.
  2. Poach the eggs.
  3. Meanwhile, remove excess fat from the bacon. Place under the grill until it's cooked to your liking.
  4. Toast the bread.
  5. When the eggs are ready and the bacon cooked, add them to the bread and enjoy.

Well done, patriot!

Just add a cup of black coffee without sugar and get ready to manifest destiny!
