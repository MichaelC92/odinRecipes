Hearty Country Hot Cakes

A thick hearty pancake that melts in your mouth!

Hot cakes are fluffy disc-shaped pancakes that you can easily make with very common and accesible ingredients. They are ideal for breakfast and will kickstart ready your body for a long day of workin' for the man. This particular recipe will yeild 6 servings that have in it all the golden country heart you can find in the American midwest. Yeeha!

In order to make them y'all need all the ingredients listed below:

  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup dry farina cereal
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Now let's get on to it. Follow the instructions listed below and you'll be all set to enjoy.

  1. Combine buttermilk, flour, sugar, farina, oil, egg, vanilla extract, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Beat using an electric mixer on low until just combined.

  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-low heat. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle and cook until middle is set, about 4 minutes. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, 3 to 5 minutes. Repeat with the remaining batter.

Well done, sugar!

Personal recommendation: pour on them some honey and add some red or blue berries on the top and it'll feel like heaven on earth!
